Detail about Jujutsu Kaisen (anime series / TV):

Description:

misery, remorse, shame: the terrible feelings that spring from people's hearts become curses that nestle in all of our lives. They leave their mark all over the world and plunge people into unspeakable misery or even death. And the only thing that can break one curse is another curse. Despite his considerable physical strength, Yuji Itadori spends his days like any other student. But one day his friend is attacked by curses and in order to save him, he eats the finger of the two-faced Sukuna and absorbs the curse into his own soul. From then on he shares his body with the double face and joins the high school for magic to fight the curses under the direction of the powerful magician Satoru Gojo. Thus begins the heroic story of the boy who became a curse to break a curse and of his life from which there is no turning back

Synopsis:

More than 10,000 dead and missing are recorded each year in Japan. The negative feelings released by human beings are involved. Suffering, regrets, humiliation: their concentration in the same place generates often fatal curses ... This is what Yuji Itadori , a high school student and member of the occult club, will discover . He does not believe in ghosts, but he is endowed with an extraordinary physical strength which represents a real asset for the missions of the group ... until one of them takes a bad turn. The relic they unearth, the severed finger of a thousand-year-old demon, attracts monsters! Without thinking: the young man swallows the relic to break the curse! Now, he finds himself possessed by Ryômen Sukuna, the famous two-faced demon. However, against all odds, Yuji is still able to maintain control of his body. But despite this, he is condemned to death by the organization of exorcists ... A decision that can only be postponed on one condition: find all of Sukuna's fingers in order to ward off the threat once and for all.
