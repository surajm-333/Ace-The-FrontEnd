Detail about Boruto: Naruto Next Generations (anime series / TV):

Description:

The village behind the leaves is in an age of peace and modernity. Huge buildings watch over the streets, gigantic screens show their colors and the thunder train rushes through the village to connect all the districts with one another. Even if it is still a village of ninjas, the population grew enormously and the life of the ninja is slowly changing. Boruto Uzumaki, the son of the Seventh Hokage Naruto Uzumaki, has enrolled at the Ninja Academy to learn the ways of the ninja. His classmates initially see him only as the "son of Hokage", but his passion and character soon blow away all prejudices. When a series of mysterious events unfold, it is up to Boruto and his friends to solve them. Like a storm, Boruto captures everyone's heart as his story begins! (Source: Crunchyroll)

Synopsis:

Since his father held the highest office in Konoha village , Boruto Uzumaki , son of Nanadaime Hokage and Hinata Hyuga, lives in the shadow of his father. Always seeking to attract the attention of the latter, Boruto made a firm resolution to surpass his father. But the life led by high-level ninjas is punctuated by complex missions and rigorous training, our young hero will also learn the hard way that becoming the best ninja is not an easy task. In the company of Sarada , the child of Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno , Boruto will therefore discover the world of shinobis, as well as its foundations ...
