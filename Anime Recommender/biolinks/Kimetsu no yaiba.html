Detail about Kimetsu no Yaiba (anime series / TV):

Description:

Tanjiro is the oldest son in a family that has lost their father. One day he visits another town to sell charcoal there, but instead of going home in the evening, he stays with someone because a rumor spreads that a demon makes the surrounding mountainous region unsafe at night. When he comes home the next day, he finds that his family has been murdered by a demon. The only survivor of the tragedy is Tanjiro's younger sister Nezuko, who, however, has become a demon. In order to make his sister human again and to take revenge on the murderer of his family, the two siblings go on a journey. An adventure story of blood and swords begins.

Synopsis:

Since ancient times, there have been rumors of man-eating demons hiding in the woods. For this reason, local townspeople never venture there at night. Legend also has it that a demon slayer roams the night, chasing away these bloodthirsty demons. For the young Tanjirô , these rumors will soon become his harsh reality ... Since the death of his father, Tanjirô has taken it upon himself to provide for his family. Despite this tragedy, they manage to find a little happiness on a daily basis. However, this little happiness is found destroyed the day Tanjirô discovers that his family has been slaughtered and that the only survivor, his sister Nezuko , has become a demon. To his surprise, Nezuko still shows signs of human emotion and thoughts. Thus begins Tanjirô's hard task , that of fighting demons and making his human sister again.
