Detail about Tensei shitara Slime Datta Ken 2nd Season Part 2 (anime series / TV):

Description:

The story takes us into the daily life of Reiji , an exploited employee who finds himself transported to another world. Once there, he will use his skills to create better potions than those existing in this world. Thanks to this, he will get a lot of money, allowing him to open a pharmacy. He will thus meet all kinds of patients, such as an elf having problems aiming with her bow or a dragon spitting fire without doing it on purpose.

Synopsis:

This is the second season of the Tensei Shitara Slime Datta Ken animated series . The Jura Tempest Federation continues to develop by trading with neighboring countries. Rimuru's goal of creating a land where humans and monsters can coexist is about to come true. However, some countries seem to retain some hostility towards monsters. While some people are plotting in the shadows, our beloved Slime is going to meet the most powerful enemy he has ever faced.
