Detail about Yuukoku no Moriarty (anime series / TV):

Description:

It's the end of the 19th century. Britain has expanded its power and reached the height of its glory amid the advancing industrial revolution. Despite all technological progress and development, the class system that has existed from ancient times is still deeply rooted. So it is still the aristocracy who rule the country, although they make up less than 3% of the population. The aristocracy enjoys its privileges as a matter of course. The lower class struggles for a living every day. All human beings are lifelong tied to the class into which they were born. William James Moriarty now sets out to smash this corrupt class system in order to build a new country based on its ideals from its ashes. Moriarty was the "Napoleon of Crime" who even made a fool of Sherlock Holmes. A revolution through crime can change the world ...

Synopsis:

Two orphaned brothers are taken in by the Moriarty family . Albert , the eldest son of the family hates the aristocracy to which he belongs and the social system that governs British society, in which the upper classes use and abuse their privileges. Albert saw in the elder the intelligence and charisma that he lacked to carry out his project of social cleansing. Albert offers to put his wealth and influence at his disposal, and in return, he will have to put his intelligence at the service of his dream of a perfect society. Thirteen years later, alongside his teaching profession, Williambecame a private consultant. With his allies, he helps people, victims of injustice, to take revenge on the bad nobles who made them suffer. Their punishment is ruthless, because the punishment they inflict is none other than ... death!
