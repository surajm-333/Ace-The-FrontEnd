Detail about Tantei wa Mou, Shindeiru. (anime series / TV):

Description:

Kimizuka Kimihiko is a magical attraction for crises. He seems to be in trouble at every turn, whether he's at a crime scene by accident or accidentally spotting a drug trade. So it's no wonder that his flight is in a state of emergency and that a detective is urgently needed on board. In his attempt to stay out of the matter, however, he is chosen by the self-proclaimed detective Siesta to be her assistant. This event is just the beginning of an adventure around the world. By putting their lives on the line, they managed to turn off criminal organizations, prevent disasters and save thousands. However, three years later, Siesta dies. Kimizuka is convinced that she can now lead a normal life. But fate seems to have other plans for him. He meets a girl who bears an uncanny resemblance to Siesta, and she threatens to throw his peaceful days upside down again.

Synopsis:

Four years ago, Kimihiko Kimitsuka started working as an assistant for a mysterious detective calling herself Siesta . Together, they traveled the world and solved all kinds of investigations while fighting the evils of a terrible secret organization. But it all ended the day Siesta died. Now Kimihiko is alone, and now he has to get back to his mundane high school life. But the task is complicated, because although the detective is dead, his past seems to want to catch up with him.
