Detail about Tsuki ga Michibiku Isekai Douchuu (anime series / TV):

Description:

High school student Misumi Makoto was suddenly called to another world by the god Tsukuyomi to be called a hero by the responsible goddess! But after arriving, a goddess awaits him who is disgusted by his appearance and explains to him that she would never give her blessing to someone so ugly. So she throws him to the edge of the world and forbids him to even approach the people of this world. After he narrowly escapes the fall from an uncertain height, he sets off alone on the journey through the wasteland without any destination or food.

Synopsis:

Based on the novel Tsuki ga Michibiku Isekai Douchuu by Azumi Kei . Misumi Makoto , an ordinary high school student, finds himself in another world after being summoned by the god Tsukuyomi in an attempt to become a hero. However, a certain goddess does not seem to want Makoto as a hero in her world and decides to propel him into the confines of it with only the ability to understand the languages ​​of non-human beings. Tsukuyomi will therefore intervene and give him some of his powers. Before disappearing, the god declares that Makoto is now free to find his own way. Now freed from all constraints, he will have to find his place in this totally unknown world.
