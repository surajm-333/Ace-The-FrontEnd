Detail about Jahy-sama wa Kujikenai! (anime series / TV):

Description:

Jahy, the demon king's adjutant, who was considered the second greatest power in the empire, ruled her subjects through fear. But when a magical girl destroys a mystical stone of Eden, the demon realm is also over. Although she survived, Jahy lost all her strength and found herself in the human world in a childlike body. In order to collect more mystical gemstones so that she can permanently restore her original shape and demon realm, she must adjust to her new life and deal with everyday problems that she has never experienced before. From working hard to cover her living expenses to arguing with the landlord when she can't pay her rent on time, Big Jahy won't be discouraged!

Synopsis:

Based on the manga Jahy-sama wa Kujikenai! by Konbu Wakame . Jahy , the feared and respected number two in the Demon Realm, suddenly finds herself helpless and shrunken in the Human Realm after a Magical Girl destroys her powerful mana crystal. We will thus follow the daily life of this demoness as she learns to live in her new environment while trying to find her original form.
