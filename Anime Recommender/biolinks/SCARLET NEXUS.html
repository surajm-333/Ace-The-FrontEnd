Detail about SCARLET NEXUS (anime series / TV):

Description:

In the distant future, a psionic hormone conferring extrasensory powers was discovered in the human brain. This discovery changed the world as we know it. However, during the year 2020 of the solar calendar, grotesque organisms called " the Others " appeared to eat the brains of human beings. To overcome this new threat, the Others Elimination Brigade (BEA) is formed. This squad is made up of people we call psionics , humans with the most powerful extrasensory abilities. Saved by this brigade as a child, Yuito Sumeragi survived training to enlist. For her part, the prodigy Kasane Randall is spotted for her abilities. However, Kasane's dreams reveal strange things to him, which will drag him, Yuito and her, towards their inescapable destiny.

Synopsis:

In a distant future, certain people will be bestowed with supernatural powers and the world is at the beginning of an unprecedented catastrophe. “The others” occupy the earth to feed on the human brain. A special unit is set up to destroy the invaders ... It is the last hope for humanity!
